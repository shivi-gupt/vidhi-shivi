SAFE PAY

Safe Pay is a value-added service offered to Shivani Mobile Center consumer durable and personal loan customers. It is package that offers Payment Holiday, Free Early Repayment and Life Cover.
Safe Pay offers three services which are as follows:
Life Cover: This allows you to avail of 1.5 times and 1.25 times the loan amount for consumer durables loan and personal loan respectively as Life Cover. In case any unanticipated calamity befalls the customer, loan obligation is covered by the insurance and nominees of the borrower are entitled to the remaining Sum Assured amount.
Payment Holiday: Payment Holiday allows customers to postpone their EMIs by one month. It can be availed after paying the first two EMIs before the due date. You can avail a maximum of two Payment Holiday Services during the entire loan term. You are required to pay at least one EMI between two Payment Holiday Services.
Free Early Repayment: Early Foreclosure allows customers to foreclose their loan without any additional charges. Customers paying EMIs through direct debit mode are required to raise the foreclosure request 5 days before the next due date. Customers paying EMIs through any other method can raise foreclosure request anytime.
